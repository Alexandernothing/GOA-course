My top 5 favorite books

top 5

Granny, Iliko, Illarion, and I

Granny, Illia, Illarion and I — Nodar Dumbadze's first and most popular novel. The main character of the work, the narrator Zurikela Vashalomidze, lives with her grandmother in the village of Khidistavi, Guria. Neighbors Iliko and Hilarion help the grandmother in raising her grandson. The action of the work takes place during the Great Patriotic War. A film of the same name was made based on the novel.

Top 4

Treasure Island

Treasure Island is the first and most popular novel of the Scottish writer Robert Louis Stevenson, published in 1883. The book tells the story of Jim Hawkins's men on an adventure with a ship's crew to Treasure Island. This journey is full of dangers and treachery.

Top 3

Robinson Crusoe

"Robinson Crusoe" is the hero of Daniel Defoe's novels. The series of novels is based on the adventures of a real person, Alexander Selkirk, who spent 28 years living on an island in the Pacific Ocean. During this time, he faced many difficulties, both from pirates and cannibals. The narration of the novels takes place in the style of a diary and creates a realistic look of a pseudo-documentary work.

Top 2

The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

"The Adventures of Tom Sawyer" is a novel by the American writer Mark Twain, which was published in 1876 and tells about the adventures of Tom Sawyer, a boy living by the Mississippi River. The action takes place in the 1840s in the local city of St. Petersburg (representing Mark Twain's hometown of Hannibal, Missouri). Although this book seems to have been overshadowed by its prequel, The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, it is considered a masterpiece of American literature.

My top 1 book

Adventures of Huckleberry Finn

The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn by Mark Twain. The novel is about a boy traveling on the Mississippi River. It was first published in the United Kingdom in 1884 and then in the United States in 1885.
